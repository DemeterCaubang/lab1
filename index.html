Hi! My name is Demeter Renee

{Deh-meh-ter Reh-nae}

Eponym

Demeter- namesake of my grandfather on my mother's side. His name is Demetrio. Demeter is the Greek "goddess of agriculture".

Renee- namesake of my grandmother on my mother's side. Her name is Rebecca. Renee is French for "reborn".

"Mind over what's the matter"

This quote is on my account description on facebook. It is a play on the classic line that anything can be overcome if one sets their mind to it.

Art Enthusiast

I consider myself an art enthusiast. The stylistic paintings, kinetic buildings, and animatic graphing easily catch my eye. Such awe-inspiring human works make me aspire to be a digital artist.

Digital Determination

However, my determination for drawing is not at the same level as my skill. I see user design as an opportunity to reach my aspiration. I aim to provide a mesmerizing yet pleasant online experience for those who would view my frontend work.
